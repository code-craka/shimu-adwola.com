2.2 Target Market Segments
Primary: Small-to-Midsize Financial Services
Accounting firms (5-50 employees), bookkeeping agencies, fractional CFO practices, and financial advisory firms. These businesses handle high-volume transactional workflows (invoicing, reconciliation, reporting) but lack dedicated automation engineers. Annual IT budgets: $10,000-$50,000.
Secondary: E-Commerce & SaaS Operations
Revenue operations teams managing subscription billing, payment reconciliation, financial reporting dashboards, and multi-currency transactions. These teams are already tool-savvy (Stripe, QuickBooks, Xero) but need integration expertise.
Tertiary: Growing Startups (Series A-B)
Companies transitioning from manual spreadsheet-based finance operations to automated systems. They need compliance-ready workflows but can’t justify a full-time automation hire.
